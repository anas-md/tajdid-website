NFC Business Card

An NFC (Near Field Communication) business card is a modern and technologically advanced version of a traditional business card. Instead of relying solely on printed information, an NFC business card allows for wireless communication with compatible devices.

NFC card offers a blend of traditional networking practices with the advantages of digital technology, making it easier for individuals to share and receive information in a quick and efficient manner.

How does it work ?

  • Provide Information

    • Users only need to prepare necessary information and links about themselves and their businesses.

  • Store data in the card

    • The acquired data is posted to a website and subsequently saved to a link on the NFC card.

  • Scan the card with your phone.

    • To access the information included in an NFC card, scan it with a smartphone or use the Quick Response Code (QR Code) supplied on the back card.

Main Features

Data Storage

Data is stored in the card using Near-Field Communication (NFC) technology, which allows users to just tap the card on a smartphone or other compatible equipment. A QR code will also be provided for users who do not have NFC.

Data Safety

The NFC card's data security technology makes it difficult for third parties to modify the data without the user's authorization.

Advantages of NFC business cards

  • Information exchange is quick and simple.
  • Eco-friendly
  • NFC data security is guaranteed.
  • QR code support for non-NFC users
  • Flexibility
