Islamic Digital Leadership Development Training

TAJDID provides an Islamic Digital Leadership Development course with the goal of establishing a competitive state based on digital innovation while simultaneously constructing a sustainable digital society.

Objectives :

  • To change the mindset of people from thinking like ordinary people to becoming extraordinary leaders at all levels.
  • Strengthen the existing leadership.
  • Develop digital leadership that applies digital usage at both organizational and individual levels.

Programme Features

  • Contains six structured modules
  • Based on Islamic leadership values, leadership philosophy, and digital innovation
  • Complete with group training
  • Evaluate the level of leadership development achievement

Advantages to Society and Organisations

Increase the competitiveness of local entrepreneurs and society in the digital economy.

Developing a qualified workforce (insan) for digital leadership

Making local communities more capable of dealing with the problems of environmental change brought about by disruptive technologies (IR 4.0).
